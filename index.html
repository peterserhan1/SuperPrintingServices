Who We Are

End-to-End Printing & Display Manufacturing

Super Printing Services is a full-service printing and display production company based in Roumieh, Lebanon. We operate a vertically integrated facility — from graphic design and high-resolution digital printing through lamination, die-cutting, and final assembly.

Our mission is to deliver retail-ready display solutions that capture attention and drive sales, combining quality craftsmanship with rapid turnaround across the MENA region.

What We Deliver

Our Services

Comprehensive display and branding solutions tailored for retail, events, and outdoor environments.

In-Store Branding

Uplift existing shelves and customize product categories in supermarkets and retail stores. We work with wood, acrylic, metal, and integrate lighting — under-shelf LEDs, light-box headers, and digital screens.

Shelf Headers Category Branding Light Boxes LED Integration

Temporary Display Stands

Cost-effective stands made from corrugated carton or corrugated plastics — ideal for short-term promotions lasting 3 to 12 months. Lightweight, easy to ship, and fully branded.

Corrugated Carton Corrugated Plastic Budget Friendly

Permanent Display Stands

Built to last up to 5 years with proper maintenance. Constructed from PVC, wood, acrylic, or metal — these stands provide a premium, long-term retail presence for your brand.

Metal PVC Wood Acrylic

Exhibitions & Events

Complete exhibition and event stand production — combining wood, metal, acrylic, and lighting to create immersive brand experiences that stand out on the show floor.

Custom Builds Modular Systems Lighting Theatres

Outdoor Posters & Vehicle Branding

Large-format outdoor posters, billboards, and full vehicle wraps. Durable vinyl printing with UV-resistant inks ensures your brand stays vibrant under any weather conditions.

Vehicle Wraps Billboards UV Resistant Large Format
